## FAQs

- **What permissions or data are required to use the AI chat?**
  - To pull in the client's context, an AI searches and synthesizes information from Gmail, Google Calendar, and Google Drive + researches the client and their company from public sources

- **What data is saved or stored by the system?**
  - AI summaries of the client's information, like purchase history or email threads or recently edited files are saved to a database, but never the actual email or file content
  - Conversations you have with the AI are stored and are visible to a small number of Athena teammates who work on the app; this is required to fix bugs, improve performance, etc

- **Can the AI overwrite, edit, or delete information like emails, invites, or files?**
  - No, the permissions are read-only; the AI cannot write email drafts, send emails, edit files, create calendar events, send meeting invitations, change calendar event descriptions, etc
